ANALYTICS

  • Web
  • Social
  • SEO
  • Data Mining
  • Email Marketing

  • Keeping track of our performance

    We carry out detailed analysis of our public websites using Google Analytics, SiteImprove and Crazy Egg. We take an in-depth look at the metrics that relate to key objectives. This gives us a complete understanding of current digital performance and highlights opportunities to improve conversion. Our reports include recommendations for enhancing the customer journey, improving retention rate and minimising drop-off prior to conversion. The results are shared with our UX designers for inclusion in their site build work.

  • Figuring out what works

    In-platform analytics allow us to review our Social Media accounts, analyzing what activity is resulting in maximum engagement, and which posts are not delivering the results required. We look to breakdown the content and images shared, as well as the timing of posts, in order to highlight themes that are encouraging likes and shares, in order to optimise future activity. We are able to investigate our follower demographic and the impact this could have on engagement, as well as using this information to help create client personas.

  • Keywords are key

    As part of our SEO focused copywriting we carry out research into keywords and phrases that are relevant to the insurance industry, product and services for use in copy, blog posts and Social Media activity. We will develop a clear, succinct and consistent message throughout by organically incorporating the terms we feel will be relevant to lead us to new clients. This is in-conjunction with any competitor analysis, as they are likely to be optimised for similar keywords and phrases, however our engaging, compelling and informative copy will ensure a clear distinction between our site and others in the industry.

  • Knowledge discovery

    We process data from different perspectives and summarize it into useful information. This information can be used to increase revenue, cut costs and find patterns within the gathered data. It enables us to determine relationships among sequential patterns to anticipate behavior patterns and trends.

  • Messages direct to the inbox

    Emails are an amazingly cost-effective way to deliver messages straight to our agents and marketers. We keep segregated contact lists to ensure our emails are relevant to our customers. Eye-catching imagery, simple headings and direct language, coupled with mobile-friendly responsiveness create a positive subscriber experience.
